How can we help?

Find answers to common questions or get in touch with our team.

How does SwapStyle AI work?

SwapStyle AI uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze the melody and structure of your uploaded song. It then "re-imagines" the track by applying the characteristics of the genre you selected, creating a completely new version while keeping the original song's essence.

Is the app free to use?

SwapStyle AI offers a free version that allows you to generate short previews. To unlock full-length song generation, higher quality audio downloads, and all premium genres, you can subscribe to our Premium plan.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed directly through your device's app store settings.

iOS: Go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > SwapStyle AI.
Android: Open Google Play Store > Tap Profile Icon > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions.

What file formats are supported?

We currently support MP3, WAV, M4A, and FLAC formats for uploading. Your swapped songs can be downloaded as high-quality MP3 or WAV files.

My generation failed, what should I do?

Rarely, complex audio files might cause the AI to timeout. Please try uploading the file again or try a different section of the song. If the problem persists, check your internet connection or contact our support team.

Our support team is just an email away. We typically reply within 24 hours.
